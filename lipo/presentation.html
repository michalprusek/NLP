Key Design Decisions

64D

Why 64D VAE Latent?

Smooth optimization surface vs 768D. KL annealing prevents collapse. Cosine loss is rotation-invariant.

10D

Why Adapter 64D ‚Üí 10D?

Efficient GP modeling, faster BoTorch. Trainable adapter learns task features. VAE stays frozen.

‚àø

Why LogEI?

Standard EI underflows for tiny improvements. Three-branch computation ensures stability near optimum.
